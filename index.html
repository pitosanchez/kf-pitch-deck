ABOUT KIDNEY FORWARD

Kidney Forward was founded in 2024 by Andrew Luu, shaped by his own journey with chronic kidney disease. After receiving a kidney from his mother at 13, Andrew found himself in need of another transplant by 2020. When family members who initially offered to donate backed out, he was placed on the waitlist in California â€” where the average wait is 8 to 10 years. That experience revealed a deeper crisis: the urgent need for more awareness and access to living kidney donation.

TToday, Kidney Forward is on a mission to eliminate transplant waitlists by activating a national network of living kidney donors. With 18 people dying every day waiting for a kidney, our work is urgent. We support patients directly, helping them find donors, improving health outcomes, and lowering long-term healthcare costs.

In 2024, Robert Sanchez joined as Director of the Jentosy Project, bringing years of lived experience and advocacy leadership. His focus on reaching underserved communities strengthens our commitment to equity and ensures that no one is left behind in the fight for a second chance at life.

COMMUNITY ENGAGEMENT AND OUTREACH

Kidney Forward's engagement strategy integrates direct support and community outreach through various channels, including social media, local television, radio broadcasts, print media, public speaking events, and active collaboration with partner organizations.

Faith Institutions

Engaging influential faith-based organizations to spread awareness and encourage community participation in organ donation.

Educational Institutions

Promoting organ donation awareness among students, nurturing the next generation's commitment to healthcare advocacy.

Health-focused Events

Targeting audiences already invested in wellness, offering a natural environment for meaningful dialogue on living kidney donation.

Barbershops and Local Businesses
